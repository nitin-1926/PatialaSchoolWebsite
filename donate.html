Do not Pity us - support us

The Deaf and Blind schools are not in receipt of any Grant from the Government or from any corporate body. We need to raise finances so that we can provide the best residential education to these deprived children.

• Sponsor Education of deaf or blind child US Dollars - 300 per year or equivalent Indian Rupees – Rs. 20000 per year (This will provide special residential education including books, stationary, uniform, medical cover, meals and daily necessities). • Share your joy and sorrows with the deaf and blind children with refreshments or meals. • Donate rations. • Donate and meet our daily needs and requirements. • Donate cash to meet our expenses.
